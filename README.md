# 🎓 AI-Based Dropout Prediction and Counseling System

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AI-Based Dropout Prediction and Counseling System** is a machine learning application designed to identify students who are at risk of dropping out from educational institutions.

The system analyzes student-related data such as academic performance, attendance, and other factors to predict the dropout risk level. Based on the prediction, institutions can take early actions such as counseling or academic support to help students continue their education.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Predict students who are at risk of dropping out.
* Help institutions identify high-risk students early.
* Support counseling and intervention strategies.
* Improve overall student retention rates.

## 🧠 Machine Learning Algorithms

The system uses machine learning techniques to classify students into different risk categories:

* Logistic Regression
* Decision Tree
* Random Forest

These models analyze historical student data and predict the likelihood of dropout.
